How do i ship my pet ?

Make sure you have all the necessary documents,such as health certificates and vaccinations. Use an appropriate crate for your pet. Choose an airline that allows pet shipping. Plan your trip carefully. Ensure your pet has food and water throughout. Familiarize yourself with the destination country's pet requirements. Make sure someone is picking up your pet at the destination.

How much does it cost to ship a pet?

The price typically ranges from several hundred to several thousand dollars, depending on factors such as size and weight, travel distance, airline and services, special requirements, and destination location. It's important to obtain quotes from pet shipping service providers and understand all associated costs before deciding to send your pet. If you would like more information, feel free to ask our team.

Do I need to provide a special cage?

You generally need to provide a special crate or container to transport your pet. This crate or container must meet specific requirements to ensure the safety of your pet during the journey. Some key points about pet shipping crates: Appropriate Size, Adequate Ventilation, Security, Labeling and Identification, Safe Flooring, Specialized Crates. Be sure to contact your pet shipping service provider to obtain the exact crate requirements tailored to your type of pet and your travel route.

What should I do after the pet arrives?

After your pet arrives, there are several steps you should take to ensure their well-being and comfort: Pick-Up, Calm Introduction, Health Check, Food and Water, Rest Time, Extra Attention, Consultation with a Veterinarian, Make sure to pay, special attention to your pet after their shipment to help them readjust well to your home. For more information, please feel free to inquire with our team.
